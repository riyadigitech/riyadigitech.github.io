Don't Skips!

Paddington Reservoir Gardens

A heritage-listed site transformed into a sunken garden with industrial ruins, hanging plants, and tranquil walkways. It’s a photographer’s dream and a quiet escape just off Oxford Street.

McMahons Point Wharf

Skip the busy Circular Quay and catch a ferry to this charming spot. The view back toward the Opera House and Harbour Bridge is arguably better—and far less crowded.

Barangaroo Reserve

Often overshadowed by Darling Harbour, Barangaroo’s sandstone paths, native gardens, and waterfront views make it a stunning place for a walk, especially at golden hour.
